Egg Salad

Boiling the eggs

Wondering how to get perfect hard-boiled eggs? Lucky for you, we’ve got a full guide for how to hard boil eggs perfectly every time. Once you’ve got the method down pat, you’ll have hard-boiled eggs to use not just in this recipe, but our classic deviled eggs too!

Mix-in variations.

The possibilities are endless when it comes to mix-ins for egg salad. Craving some extra tang? Throw in some chopped pickles or capers. Play with the herbs by adding in some dill, parsley, or tarragon (really, any soft herb that you love is worth trying). If your egg salad is lacking some spice, black pepper or hot sauce will be your new best friends. You could even add a pinch of curry powder if you want! Whether you go classic or get creative, the most important thing is to make your egg salad how YOU like it.

Ingredients

  • 8 hard-boiled eggs, peeled
  • 1/2 stalk celery, finely chopped (about 1/3 c.)
  • 1/4 c. mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp. finely sliced chives
  • 2 tsp. fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tsp. whole-grain mustard
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Paprika and crackers, for serving

Recipe instructions

  1. Coarsely chop eggs and transfer to a medium bowl. Slightly mash with a fork to break up yolks. Add celery, mayonnaise, chives, lemon juice, and mustard and mix until combined; season with salt and pepper.
  2. Transfer egg salad to a serving bowl. Top with paprika. Serve with crackers alongside.
